Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 min (prep and bake)
  • Serves: 36 cookies
  • Calories: 183 cal
Equipment: Measuring spoons Measuring cups KitchenAid mixer Spatula Baking sheet

Steps:
Preheat oven: 375 degrees
Bowl 1: Mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder
Bowl 2: Beat eggs and vanilla until fluffy, Mix in dry ingredients until combined
Roll dough onto baking sheet, bake for 8-10 minutes

Source: Chocolate chip cookie recipe

Recipe #2

  • Time: 3 hours, 25 min (prep, bake, and clean up)
  • Serves: 12-14 cupcakes
Equipment: Measuring spoons Measuring cups KitchenAid mixer Frosting bag and tip Cupcake baking sheet

Steps:
Preheat oven: 350 degrees(Line sheet with cupcake liners)
Bowl 1: Mix flour, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, and baking soda
Bowl 2:Whisk eggs sugar, brown sugar, oil, and vanilla
Pour half of the wet and dry ingredients into a bowl,add buttermilk, then pour in the other half.
Pour batter into cupcake liners, bake for 18-21 minutes

Source: Chocolate cupcake recipe

Recipe #3

  • Time: 1 hour (prep and bake)
  • Serves: 16 brownies
Equipment: Measuring spoons Measuring cups Bowl and whisk Baking sheet

Steps:
Preheat oven: 350 degrees
1st: Melt butter, stir in sugar,eggs, and vanilla
2nd: Stir in cocoa, flour, salt and baking powder
Butter the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes
Source: Chocolate brownie recipe
